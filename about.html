My Story

Hello! I'm Wetaka Ivan, a dedicated education student at the prestigious Islamic University in Uganda (IUIU). I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science Education with a double specialization in Mathematics and Information Communication Technology (ICT).

My educational journey began in the beautiful Sironko district, moving through various institutions that each contributed uniquely to my growth. From Sironko Demonstration School to Nkoma Secondary School, I've experienced diverse learning environments that have shaped my understanding of Uganda's educational landscape.

What drives me is the belief that every Ugandan student deserves access to quality mathematics education combined with modern technological skills. I'm not just training to be a teacher; I'm preparing to be an educational innovator who bridges the gap between traditional teaching methods and the digital future.

Beyond academics, I'm a continuous learner with certifications from Cisco Networking Academy and Educate Uganda. I believe in the power of education to transform lives and communities, and I'm committed to being part of Uganda's educational renaissance.
